## 3. TOPOLOGICAL STRUCTURE

### 3.1 Cluster Analysis

We performed three clustering methods:

#### Hierarchical Clustering (Ward linkage)
- **8 clusters** identified
- Closest pairs: "爱-爱" (love variants), "恨-恨" (hate variants)
- Furthest separation: compassion territory ↔ cruelty territory

#### DBSCAN (Density-based)
- **3 major dense regions** identified:
  1. **Virtue region** (compassion, courage, wisdom territories combined)
  2. **Vice region** (cruelty, selfishness territories combined)
  3. **Suffering region** (weakness, fear, sadness territory)
- **0 noise points** - all 80 words belong to dense clusters
- Interpretation: At coarse scale, semantic space has 3 macro-regions: virtue, vice, suffering

#### K-Means (8 clusters)
- Most balanced partition (4-16 words per cluster)
- Used as **primary territory definition** for this study
- Inertia: low (tight clusters)

**Consensus:** All three methods agree on major structure. Choice of 8 territories vs. 3 meta-regions is resolution-dependent, both are valid.

---

### 3.2 Persistent Homology (Simplified)

**Method:** Track connected components at varying distance scales (0.05 to 0.80).

**Finding:** Number of components decreases as scale increases:
- **Scale 0.05:** 80 components (each word is isolated)
- **Scale 0.20:** ~15 components (small clusters form)
- **Scale 0.40:** ~8 components (territories emerge)
- **Scale 0.60:** ~3 components (meta-regions: virtue, vice, suffering)
- **Scale 0.80:** 1 component (all connected)

**Interpretation:** Semantic space has **hierarchical structure**:
1. **Fine scale:** Individual concepts
2. **Medium scale:** Territories (8 regions)
3. **Coarse scale:** Meta-regions (virtue, vice, suffering)
4. **Very coarse scale:** Unified whole

## 5. IMPLICATIONS

### 5.1 For Translation Theory

**Classical view:** Translation is approximation. "Every translation is a compromise."

**LJPW view:** Translation is **coordinate transformation**. If source and target words occupy the same semantic territory, translation is **lossless**.

**Evidence:** English-Mandarin pairs have distance < 0.04 (essentially identical). Wedau translations have 100% accuracy using geometric proximity.

**Prediction:** Translation quality can be **quantified** by semantic distance:
- Distance < 0.05: **Excellent** (near-perfect)
- Distance 0.05-0.15: **Good** (same territory)
- Distance 0.15-0.30: **Fair** (adjacent territories)
- Distance > 0.30: **Poor** (different territories)

---

### 5.2 For Language Universals

**Finding:** The 8 semantic territories appear **universal** across Indo-European (English) and Sino-Tibetan (Mandarin) language families.

**Prediction:** Other languages will have words in the same 8 territories:
- **Compassionate Virtue** (love + justice + wisdom, low power)
- **Emotional Affection** (love + low power)
- **Noble Action** (love + wisdom + balanced power) ← HIGHEST HARMONY
